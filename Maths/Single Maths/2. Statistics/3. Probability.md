## 3.2 Conditional Probability

### Formulae

* $P(A') = 1-P(A)$.
* $P(A\cup B)=P(A)+P(B) - P(A\cap B)$.
* $P(A\cap B)=P(A)\cdot P(B|A)$.
* $P(A|B)=\frac{P(A\cap B)}{P(B)}$.
