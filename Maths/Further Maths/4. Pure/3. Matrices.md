## Matrix addition and Multiplication

### Addition

$$
\begin{pmatrix}a&b\\c&d \end{pmatrix}+\begin{pmatrix}e&f\\g&h\end{pmatrix}=\begin{pmatrix}(a+e)&(b+f)\\(c+g)&(d+h)\end{pmatrix}
$$

* Matrix addition is commutative and associative.
